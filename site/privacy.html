[Draft] Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 12/18/2018

Use

Tonic has no intention of using your stored data in any way outside of using it to accurately invoke jobs on your repositories.

Data Storage

Tonic uses third party vendors and hosting partners to store data. Although Tonic owns the code, databases, and all rights to the application, you retain all rights to your data.

In order for Tonic to function, it must store some information about your repositories. Here is a list of the information stored:

  • Repository name
  • Repository owner's username

Questions

Please contact tonic@dawnlabs.io if you have any questions.
